The great outdoor adventures

Peru, the third largest country in South America, known as the "Land of Incas" is a multicultural country, full of traditions, an award-winning gastronomy and vast natural reserves. Located in the western part of South America and shares its borders with Ecuador, Colombia, Brazil, Bolivia and Chile. Its territory, of more than 1.2 million km², covers three regions: Coast, Highlands and Jungle.

Cities and their activities

Find the destination you would like to visit

  • Mancora

    Visit the beautiful beaches of Mancora and its environment with its famous turtles.

  • Arequipa

    The city called "white city" is firmly guarded by its most imposing natural attraction: the Misti volcano.

  • Cusco - Plaza Mayor

    If you are interested in the history of the Incas, we recommend you to visit the city.

  • Lima

    Visit the capital of Peru so famous for its cultural activities, its districts of Miraflores and San Isidro, which are located near the sea.

  • Tacna

    Historical city for the war between Peru and Chile with Boloñesi. Located near the desert, it has beautiful landscapes.

  • Rainbow Mountain

    Also called Montaña de Siete Colores, is a mountain in the Andes of Peru with an altitude of 5,200 metres (17,100 ft) above sea level.
